About Our MFI Network

TESAA partners with a diverse range of microfinance institutions (MFIs) to provide accessible financial services to underserved communities. Our network includes both established and emerging MFIs, all committed to fostering economic growth and financial inclusion.

Our MFI Partners

Faulu Microfinance Bank

Provides a wide range of financial products for individuals and SMEs.
